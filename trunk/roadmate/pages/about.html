about us

RoadMate is a carpooling web site providing you with the opportunity to share car rides New Zealand wide. You can enjoy rides as a passenger or save yourself a few bucks by helping others to get places. Find out more on the Support page.

Our goal is to save petrol, save you money, reduce heavy traffic and keep New Zealand green. We hope you'll all have wonderful trips and make some new RoadMates.
