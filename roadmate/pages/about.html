about us

RoadMate is a carpooling web site providing you with the opportunity to share car rides NewZealandWide. You can enjoy rides as a passenger or save yourself a few bucks by helping oters to get places. More on the Support page

Our goal is to save petrol, save you money, reduce havey traffic and keep NewZealand green. We hope you'll all have wonderful trips and make new RoadMates.
